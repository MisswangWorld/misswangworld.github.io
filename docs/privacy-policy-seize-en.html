Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

Introduction

Seize is a focus timer application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally

The app only stores the following data on your device:

  • Focus Records: Start time, end time, duration, and tags
  • Tag History: Tags you have used
  • App Settings: Your preferences

This data is never transmitted to external servers.

iOS Calendar Access

With your permission, the app accesses iOS Calendar to:

  • Create a "Seize" calendar to store focus records
  • Write focus events to your selected calendar
  • Read calendar names to display available calendars

Notifications

With your permission, the app sends local notifications to remind you when focus time ends. These notifications are processed entirely on your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect usage analytics
  • We do not use third-party tracking or advertising services
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data by:

  • Deleting the app from your device
  • Clearing data in iOS Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Seize

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages and operates without requiring any personal information.

Your data stays on your device — we do not collect or transmit any personal information.
